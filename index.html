A bit about me…

Discovering the transformative power of art shattered the notion that it was merely a hobby. Rooted in my diverse heritage—American, Yaqui, Welsh, Italian, and Spanish—I’ve forged a path fueled by creativity and resilience. Each day presents new challenges which I embrace, weaving design, photography, painting, and more into my exploration. Inspired by nature’s beauty, poetry now speaks to my soul.

With over two decades of experience spanning robotics to retail, I’ve thrived in high-stakes environments, collaborating with prestigious clients. From concept to execution, I specialize in crafting impactful narratives across mediums. I am a visionary leader, unafraid to challenge conventions, seeking not just success but significance in every endeavor.
